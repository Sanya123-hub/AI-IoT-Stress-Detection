[ 01 ] GSR — top predictor

49.9% feature importance

Galvanic Skin Response directly measures sympathetic nervous system arousal — the most reliable single indicator of psychological stress, confirmed by psychophysiology research.

[ 02 ] Stable generalisation

Only 0.4% CV gap

Test accuracy (88.0%) vs 5-fold cross-validation (88.4%) gap of just 0.4% — confirming the model is not overfitting despite a small 25-participant dataset.

[ 03 ] Affordable hardware

~₹1,000–1,500 per unit

Total device cost roughly USD 12–18 using consumer-grade sensors. Practical for large-scale deployment in community health centres and school counselling services.
